Features

Everything Your Society Needs

A comprehensive platform to manage access, residents, visitors, and more with a single solution.

Smart Door Locks

IoT-enabled smart locks with remote access control. Lock and unlock doors from your phone, generate temporary access codes for guests.

Visitor Management

Pre-approve visitors, generate QR-based entry passes, and track every entry and exit in real-time with photo verification.

Resident Management

Manage resident profiles, flat assignments, family members, and move-in/move-out workflows with approval processes.

Society & Tower Setup

Multi-society support with towers, floors, and flats. Configurable flat types (1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK) and ownership tracking.

Admin Dashboard

Real-time analytics with society stats, resident counts, subscription tracking, and revenue reports at your fingertips.

Notifications & Alerts

Push notifications for visitor arrivals, access events, maintenance requests, and important society announcements.
